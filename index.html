About Us

Tino Eco is a youth-led environmental nonprofit that strives to spread climate literacy and engagement on a community level by providing a network of resources and access to opportunities to tackle environmental doomism. This can look like providing articles on environmental topics, local initiatives, or news; notes for useful environment educational endeavors; a lens into influential minds through podcasts; a database of environmental organizations and their goals as well as work; local consistent park volunteering, and more.
